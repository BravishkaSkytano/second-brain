> [!tldr] Byzantine art
> Refers to the artwork of the [[Eastern Roman Empire]], and the nations and states that inherited the culture.
> 
> Characterized as abstract with anti-naturalistic character and symbolism.
> 
> ![[Byzantine art-1760304037177.webp|Christ Pantocrator|500x529]]
> 

^f45b4c

The basis of Byzantine art is the fundamental artistic attitude of the Byzantine Greeks who loved to give meaningful content to different forms of life.

![[Byzantine art-1760304097954.webp|Enthroned Virgin and Child with saints and angels|500x722]]

Byzantine artwork is still maintained in Eastern Orthodox countries, specifically Greece, Cyprus, Serbia, Bulgaria, Romania, and Russia.
