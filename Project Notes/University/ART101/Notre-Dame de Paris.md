A famous **[[Gothic art|Gothic]] cathedral** in Paris, France, begun in 1163 and mostly completed by 1345. It is known for its **pointed arches**, **[[Flying Buttress|flying buttress]]es**, **soaring towers**, **stained glass windows** (like the [[Rose Window]]), and **sculptural decoration** depicting biblical scenes.

**Example artwork/feature:** The **flying buttresses** and the **Rose Window** are iconic elements of Gothic architecture at Notre-Dame.
