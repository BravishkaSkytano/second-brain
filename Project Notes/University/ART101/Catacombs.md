> [!tldr] Catacombs
> Underground **burial chambers** used by early Christians (and sometimes Jews) from the **2nd to 5th centuries CE**. They were dug beneath cities like **Rome** and included **tunnels, tombs, and small chapels** decorated with **frescoes** showing **biblical scenes** and symbols of faith.

^042d68

**Example:** The **Catacombs of Priscilla** in **Rome**, known for early Christian wall paintings like **“The Good Shepherd.”**
