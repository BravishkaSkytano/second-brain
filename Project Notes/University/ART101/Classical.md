> [!summary] Classical
> 
> *Adjective*.
> 
> 1. Relating to the ancient Greeks and Romans, namely their art, architecture, and literature.
> 2. Conforming to the artistic and literary models of ancient Greece and Rome.
> 3. Educated in the classics, e.g. "a classical scholar."
