This course will introduce the student primarily to the history and theology of the religion of Islam. Special emphasis will be given to topics such as the life of Mohammad, the Qur'an and the "Golden Age of Islam" (10th-13th centuries). The course will also include introductory information on Christian apologetics to Muslims.
