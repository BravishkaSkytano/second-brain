> [!tldr] Rose Window
> A large, **circular stained glass window** with **intricate designs** that resemble the petals of a rose. Common in **[[Gothic art|Gothic]] cathedrals**, rose windows often depict **biblical scenes or saints** and fill the interior with **colorful light**.
