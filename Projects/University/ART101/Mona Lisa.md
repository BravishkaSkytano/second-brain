> [!tldr] Mona Lisa
> Painting by [[Leonardo da Vinci]]; known for her mysterious smile and realistic background.
> ![[Mona Lisa-500x745.webp|250x373]]

^741fea

The *Mona Lisa* is a renowned portrait painting by [[Leonardo da Vinci]]. It was created between 1503 and 1517 and acquired by King Francis I of France. It is believed that the subject of the painting is Lisa del Giocondo. Currently, the Mona Lisa is displayed at the Louvre as one of the most famous and valuable artworks in the world.
