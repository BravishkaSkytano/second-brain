- Broad term — refers to any creative visual piece made by an artist.
- Includes paintings, sculptures, architecture, drawings, engravings, mosaics, tapestries, etc.
- Example: Michelangelo’s _David_ (a sculpture) is an _artwork_, but not a _painting_.
