> [!tldr] Apse
> A semicircular or polygonal recess at the end of a church, usually behind the altar. It often has a domed or [[Vault|vault]]ed ceiling and serves as a focal point for worship, sometimes decorated with [[Mosaic|mosaic]]s or [[Fresco|fresco]]es.

^552857

**Example:** The apse [[Mosaic|mosaic]] in the Church of San Vitale in Ravenna, Italy.
