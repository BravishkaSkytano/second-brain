> [!summary] Kouros
> A free-standing sculpture of a nude male youth, typically made of marble or bronze, produced in ancient Greece during the [[Archaic]] period. They're characterized by their rigid, stylized poses, with one foot slightly forward and arms held straight down.

^0c1f70

Kouroi were often used as statues of young men or as offerings to deities. They represent an beginning stage in the development of Greek sculpture, influenced by Egyptian and Near Eastern artistic traditions.

[Archaic]: Archaic "Archaic"
