> [!tldr] Nave
> The **central main area** of a church where the **congregation sits or stands** during services. It usually runs from the **entrance** to the **altar**, often flanked by **side aisles** separated by rows of columns or arches.

^c376ab

**Example:** The **nave of Chartres Cathedral** in **France**, known for its soaring height and stained glass windows.
